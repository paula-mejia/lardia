# Licenças e Afastamentos — Leave & Absences

## Licença-Maternidade

### Duration
- **120 days** (minimum)
- Can extend **+15 days** for health reasons (with medical certificate)
- Starts: up to 28 days before due date OR from birth date
- Adoption: same 120 days from judicial custody date

### Salário-Maternidade
- Paid by **INSS** (Previdência Social), NOT by employer
- Value: equal to last registered salary
- Employer continues to deposit FGTS during leave

### Stability
- Employee has job stability from **confirmation of pregnancy until 5 months after birth**
- Cannot be dismissed during this period (except justa causa)
- If dismissed without knowledge of pregnancy: must be reinstated

### How to Request
- Employee requests via Meu INSS (app or website)
- Or at INSS agency with medical documentation
- Required: birth certificate or medical certificate with due date

## Auxílio-Doença

### Two Types
1. **Previdenciário** — illness/accident NOT related to work
   - No job stability after return
   - Employer CAN dismiss after return
2. **Acidentário** — work-related illness/accident
   - **12 months job stability** after return
   - Employer deposits FGTS during entire leave

### Key Rules
- Paid by **INSS from day 1** of incapacity (unlike CLT workers where employer pays first 15 days)
- This is a special rule for domestic workers
- Contract is **suspended** during leave (no salary from employer)
- Employee must file via Meu INSS with medical documentation

### Requirements
- Minimum 12 months of INSS contributions (carência)
- Exception: no carência for accidents or serious diseases on specific list
- Medical exam/pericia at INSS to confirm incapacity

### Employer Obligations During Leave
- **Previdenciário**: no FGTS, no salary obligations
- **Acidentário**: must continue depositing FGTS
- Cannot alter or terminate contract during leave

## Atestado Médico (Medical Certificate)

### Absence Justification
- Medical certificate (atestado) justifies absence and **excuses the day** (abona falta)
- Employee should present within 48h when possible
- Employer cannot refuse a valid medical certificate
- Up to 15 days: employer accepts atestado, no INSS involvement
- 16+ days: employee must request auxílio-doença from INSS

### What Makes a Valid Atestado
- Doctor's name and CRM number
- Date and duration of leave
- CID code (diagnosis) — optional but common
- Doctor's signature and stamp

## Other Justified Absences (Faltas Justificadas)

Per CLT, employee may be absent without salary deduction:
- **2 days**: death of spouse, parent, child, sibling, dependent
- **3 days**: marriage
- **1 day**: child birth (for father — in addition to 5-day paternity leave)
- **1 day/year**: blood donation
- **Court dates**: as needed with proof
- **Military service obligations**
- **Election days** (when applicable)

## Licença-Paternidade
- **5 consecutive days** from child's birth
- Paid by employer (not INSS)
- Applies to biological and adoptive fathers

## CAT (Comunicação de Acidente de Trabalho)
- Must be filed via eSocial within **1 business day** of accident
- Or immediately in case of death
- Required for employee to access acidentário benefits

### Licença-Maternidade — Additional Details
- **Aborto espontâneo or non-criminal**: 14 days of leave (not 120)
- **Natimorto** (stillbirth from 20th week): full 120 days
- **Adoção**: applies to children up to 12 years old; starts from judicial custody date
- **Guarda para fins de adoção**: also triggers 120 days
- Employer may grant vacation to "bridge" with maternity leave (emenda), as long as the employee has completed the aquisitive period
- **Casais homoafetivos**: same maternity leave rights apply
- **Employee death during maternity leave**: spouse/partner may receive the remaining benefit

### Salário-Maternidade — Who Pays What
- INSS pays the salário-maternidade directly to the employee (via Meu INSS request)
- Employer does NOT pay salary during the leave, but must continue paying:
  - FGTS (8% + 3.2%) via DAE monthly
  - Since Dec 2020 (STF decision): employer is **exempt from INSS patronal** on salário-maternidade
- No carência (waiting period) for salário-maternidade — but employee must be contributing to INSS
- Value: equal to the last registered salary

### Pregnancy Stability — Extended Rules
- Stability applies even if employer didn't know about the pregnancy at time of dismissal
- If conception occurred during the employment contract, employee has retroactive stability rights
- Applies to: prazo indeterminado, contrato de experiência, and contratos temporários
- Stability also covers aviso prévio (trabalhado or indenizado)
- Dismissal of pregnant employee (even unknowingly) → must reinstate or pay all wages through the stability period

### Licença Amamentação
- After returning from maternity leave, employee is entitled to **two 30-minute breaks per day** for breastfeeding until the child is 6 months old
- These breaks are paid and count as work time

### Auxílio-Doença — Domestic Worker Special Rule
- Unlike CLT workers where employer pays the first 15 days: for domestic workers, **INSS pays from day 1**
- This is a unique benefit for the domestic worker category
- Practical approach: for short absences (a few days), employer typically accepts atestado and excuses the absence without INSS involvement
- For longer absences: employee must file via Meu INSS for the benefit
- During auxílio-doença: employer may hire a diarista (≤2 days/week) as temporary replacement

### Acidente de Trabalho — Common Domestic Accidents
- Falls (escorregões), burns (cooking), cuts, chemical exposure (cleaning products), repetitive strain injuries
- Even a minor slip can be classified as a workplace accident if it causes incapacity
- Employer has **48 hours** to file CAT (not 1 business day for regular accidents — immediate only for death)
- Employee maintains insurance via the 0.8% SAT paid monthly in the DAE
- Employer's responsibility: provide safe working conditions, adequate supplies, and first aid when needed

### Estabilidade do Empregado Doméstico
- **Two situations** guarantee provisional stability:
  1. Licença-maternidade: from pregnancy confirmation until 5 months after birth
  2. Auxílio-doença acidentário: 12 months of stability after return to work
- Auxílio-doença previdenciário (non-work-related illness): NO stability guarantee
- Regional conventions (convenções coletivas) may add additional stability protections — check local sindicato

### Additional Justified Absences (from NoLar, complementing CLT Art. 473)
- Medical consultation for child under 6: 1 day/year
- Preventive cancer exams (with proof): 3 days per 12 months
- Prenatal consultations during pregnancy: 2 days
