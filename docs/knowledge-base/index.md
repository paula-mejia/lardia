## Key Legislation

- **Lei Complementar nº 150/2015** — Main law regulating domestic employment
- **CLT** — Consolidação das Leis Trabalhistas (supplementary)
- **Emenda Constitucional nº 72/2013** — PEC das Domésticas (constitutional amendment)
- **Lei nº 8.036/90** — FGTS regulations

## Who is a Domestic Worker?

Per LC 150/2015: anyone who provides continuous, subordinate, paid, personal services with no profit purpose to a person or family in a residential setting, **more than 2 days per week**. Includes: housekeepers, cooks, nannies, elderly caregivers, drivers, gardeners, doormen, cleaners.

## Key 2026 Updates
- **Salário mínimo 2026**: R$ 1.621,00 (Decreto Federal nº 12.797/2025, effective 01/01/2026)
- **Reajuste**: 6.78% over 2025 value of R$ 1.518,00
- States with regional floors (SP, RJ, SC, PR, RS) may have higher values
- All employer costs (FGTS, INSS, etc.) recalculated on new base
