# Rescisão — Termination Rules

## Types of Termination

### 1. Demissão sem Justa Causa (Dismissal without cause)
Employer's initiative, no fault by employee.
**Employee receives:**
- Saldo de salário (remaining salary)
- Aviso prévio (indenizado or trabalhado)
- Férias vencidas + proporcionais + 1/3
- 13° proporcional
- **FGTS 40% fine** on total deposits
- Right to withdraw FGTS
- Right to seguro-desemprego (3 installments of 1 minimum wage)

### 2. Demissão por Justa Causa (Dismissal with cause)
Employee committed serious fault (Art. 482 CLT).
**Employee receives only:**
- Saldo de salário
- Férias vencidas + 1/3 (if any)
- No FGTS withdrawal, no 40% fine, no seguro-desemprego

### 3. Pedido de Demissão (Employee resignation)
Employee's initiative.
**Employee receives:**
- Saldo de salário
- Férias vencidas + proporcionais + 1/3
- 13° proporcional
- No FGTS withdrawal, no 40% fine, no seguro-desemprego
- Employee must give 30 days notice (or employer can deduct)

### 4. Rescisão por Acordo (Mutual agreement — Art. 484-A CLT)
Both parties agree to end contract.
**Employee receives:**
- Saldo de salário
- 50% of aviso prévio (if indenizado)
- Férias vencidas + proporcionais + 1/3
- 13° proporcional
- **FGTS 20% fine** (half the normal)
- Can withdraw 80% of FGTS balance
- No seguro-desemprego

### 5. Término de Contrato por Prazo Determinado
Contract expires naturally.
- Saldo de salário, férias proporcionais + 1/3, 13° proporcional
- No aviso prévio, no 40% fine

## FGTS 40% Fine

- Applies only on dismissal without cause
- Calculated on **total FGTS deposits** during contract (updated with interest)
- The 3.2% FGTS Compensatório deposited monthly serves as provision for this fine
- Paid via eSocial/GRRF

## Aviso Prévio

- Minimum 30 days
- Additional 3 days per year of service (max 90 days total)
- Can be **trabalhado** (worked) or **indenizado** (paid without working)
- During worked notice: employee can choose 2h/day reduction OR 7 days off

## Termination Deadlines
- Payment of all amounts: **10 days** from end of contract
- Late payment: fine of 1 monthly salary to the employee

## eSocial Process
- Register termination in eSocial with motive code
- Generate GRRF (if applicable) for FGTS fine
- System calculates all amounts due

### Saldo de Salário Calculation
- Formula: (salary ÷ 30) × days worked in the final month
- Always use **calendar days** (dias corridos), NOT business days

### FGTS Compensatório — How It Works in Practice
- The 3.2% monthly deposit is NOT a "40% fine" — it is a **substitute mechanism** unique to domestic work
- In other sectors, employer pays 40% fine on total FGTS at termination; domestic workers get the 3.2% monthly provision instead
- **Who gets the 3.2% accumulated:**
  - Demissão sem justa causa → employee gets 100%
  - Rescisão por culpa recíproca → employee gets 50%
  - Rescisão por acordo → employee gets 50%
  - Pedido de demissão → employer can withdraw (saque at Caixa)
  - Justa causa → employer can withdraw
  - Falecimento → employer can withdraw (not passed to dependents)
- To withdraw: go to Caixa Econômica with RG, CPF, proof of address, TRCT

### Rescisão por Acordo — Additional Details
- Introduced by Reforma Trabalhista (Lei 13.467/2017), applied to domestic workers via CLT subsidiária
- Before the reform, the only options were: sem justa causa, justa causa, or pedido de demissão
- The agreement formalizes a practice that was already common informally
- **Key restriction**: employee loses right to seguro-desemprego in this modality

### Termination Due to Employee Death (Falecimento)
- Death causes **automatic contract termination**
- Employer must pay within 10 days (from presentation of documents by dependents, per TST understanding)
- Verbas due: saldo de salário, férias proporcionais + 1/3, férias vencidas + 1/3, 13° proporcional, salário-família proporcional
- NOT due: aviso prévio (not applicable), FGTS 40% fine
- FGTS Compensatório (3.2%): employer can withdraw the full accumulated amount
- FGTS regular (8%): dependents can access via Caixa with habilitação
- Dependents must be legally qualified (habilitação) to receive the payments

### eSocial Rescisão Module
- eSocial automatically calculates: saldo de salário, aviso prévio indenizado, 13°, férias proporcionais, terço constitucional, salário-família
- Items requiring **manual entry**: horas extras, desconto de faltas, multa por atraso, other specific rubricas
- System generates the TRCT (Termo de Rescisão do Contrato de Trabalho)

### Recontratação (Rehiring) Rules
- After demissão sem justa causa: must wait **90 days** before rehiring the same employee (Portaria 384/1992)
- After demissão por justa causa or pedido de demissão: no waiting period (no FGTS/seguro-desemprego fraud risk)
- New contract requires fresh registration in eSocial and CTPS with new admission date
- If termination was NOT yet processed in eSocial: employer can simply cancel the termination instead of rehiring
